How to Pitch

The five things to lead with.

Use these talking points to frame FCI for a candidate. Adapt them to their background, but keep the conversation centered on model, market, and operator fit.

01

The mobile flooring showroom

No retail buildout. Owners run the business from a small office with a branded vehicle that carries thousands of samples to the customer's home.

02

Recession-resilient category

Flooring is repair and replace, not discretionary. Homeowners delay, but do not skip, needed flooring updates.

03

Built for non-flooring operators

Most FCI owners had never sold flooring before signing. The system is designed for operators who can lead people, process, and customer experience.

04

Marketing handled centrally

Owners are not marketers. The field marketing team supports each location with digital, local, and lead-generation campaigns.

05

Strong unit economics

Reference Item 19 in the FDD for current ACR, AUV, and owner P&L ranges. Always direct candidates to the FDD for numbers, not quoted averages out of context.
